authors: Danilo Arnone, ..., Sudhakar Selvaraj
journal: Neuroscience and Behavioral Reviews
title: "Role of Kynurenine pathway and its metabolites in mood disorders: A systematic review and meta-analysis of clinical studies"
note:
- systematic review
- meta analysis of kynurenine pathway metabolites in Unipolar Depression and in Bipolar Disorder
- - kynurenine, kynurenic acid, tryptophan, quinolinic acid
- decrease in kynurenine in Unipolar Major Depression
- no significant differences in Bipolar Disorder
